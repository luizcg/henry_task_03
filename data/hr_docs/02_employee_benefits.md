# Employee Benefits Guide - Acme Corporation

## Benefits Overview

Acme Corporation is committed to providing comprehensive benefits that support the health, financial security, and well-being of our employees and their families. This guide outlines all benefits available to eligible employees.

## Eligibility

### Full-Time Employees

Full-time employees (working 30+ hours per week) are eligible for all benefits:
- Medical, dental, and vision insurance
- Life and disability insurance
- 401(k) retirement plan
- Employee assistance program
- All voluntary benefits

### Part-Time Employees

Part-time employees (working 20-29 hours per week) are eligible for:
- 401(k) retirement plan (after 1 year)
- Employee assistance program
- Limited voluntary benefits

### Eligibility Waiting Period

Benefits eligibility begins:
- **Medical/Dental/Vision**: First of the month following 30 days of employment
- **401(k)**: First of the month following 90 days of employment
- **Life/Disability**: First of the month following 30 days of employment

## Health Insurance

### Medical Plans

Acme Corporation offers three medical plan options:

#### PPO Plan (Preferred Provider Organization)
- **Monthly Premium**: Employee only $150, Employee + Spouse $350, Family $500
- **Deductible**: $500 individual, $1,000 family
- **Out-of-Pocket Maximum**: $3,000 individual, $6,000 family
- **Primary Care Visit**: $25 copay
- **Specialist Visit**: $50 copay
- **Emergency Room**: $250 copay
- **Prescription Drugs**: $10 generic, $35 preferred brand, $60 non-preferred

#### HMO Plan (Health Maintenance Organization)
- **Monthly Premium**: Employee only $100, Employee + Spouse $250, Family $375
- **Deductible**: $250 individual, $500 family
- **Out-of-Pocket Maximum**: $2,000 individual, $4,000 family
- **Primary Care Visit**: $20 copay
- **Specialist Visit**: $40 copay (referral required)
- **Emergency Room**: $200 copay
- **Prescription Drugs**: $5 generic, $25 preferred brand, $50 non-preferred

#### HDHP Plan (High Deductible Health Plan)
- **Monthly Premium**: Employee only $50, Employee + Spouse $125, Family $200
- **Deductible**: $1,500 individual, $3,000 family
- **Out-of-Pocket Maximum**: $5,000 individual, $10,000 family
- **HSA Eligible**: Yes, company contributes $500/year to HSA
- **All Services**: Subject to deductible, then 80% covered

### Dental Insurance

#### Dental PPO Plan
- **Monthly Premium**: Employee only $25, Employee + Spouse $50, Family $75
- **Preventive Care**: 100% covered (cleanings, x-rays, exams)
- **Basic Services**: 80% covered after $50 deductible (fillings, extractions)
- **Major Services**: 50% covered after $50 deductible (crowns, bridges, dentures)
- **Orthodontia**: 50% covered, $2,000 lifetime maximum (dependents under 19)
- **Annual Maximum**: $2,000 per person

### Vision Insurance

#### Vision Plan
- **Monthly Premium**: Employee only $10, Employee + Spouse $20, Family $30
- **Eye Exam**: $10 copay, once per year
- **Frames**: $150 allowance, once per year
- **Lenses**: Covered in full, once per year
- **Contact Lenses**: $150 allowance in lieu of frames/lenses
- **LASIK Discount**: 15% off at participating providers

## Life and Disability Insurance

### Basic Life Insurance

Provided at no cost to employees:
- **Coverage Amount**: 1x annual salary (up to $500,000)
- **Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D)**: Equal to life insurance amount
- **Beneficiary**: Designated by employee in HR Portal

### Supplemental Life Insurance

Optional additional coverage:
- **Employee**: Up to 5x annual salary (guaranteed issue up to 3x)
- **Spouse**: Up to $250,000 (guaranteed issue up to $50,000)
- **Children**: $10,000 per child
- **Rates**: Based on age, see rate table in HR Portal

### Short-Term Disability (STD)

Provided at no cost to employees:
- **Benefit**: 60% of weekly salary
- **Maximum Benefit**: $2,500 per week
- **Waiting Period**: 7 days
- **Benefit Duration**: Up to 12 weeks

### Long-Term Disability (LTD)

Provided at no cost to employees:
- **Benefit**: 60% of monthly salary
- **Maximum Benefit**: $10,000 per month
- **Waiting Period**: 90 days (after STD exhausted)
- **Benefit Duration**: Until age 65 or recovery

## Retirement Benefits

### 401(k) Plan

#### Employee Contributions
- **Contribution Limits**: Up to IRS maximum ($22,500 for 2024, $30,000 if age 50+)
- **Pre-Tax or Roth**: Employee choice
- **Contribution Changes**: Can be made anytime via HR Portal

#### Company Match
- **Match Formula**: 100% match on first 3% + 50% match on next 2%
- **Maximum Match**: 4% of eligible compensation
- **Vesting Schedule**: 
  - Year 1: 25% vested
  - Year 2: 50% vested
  - Year 3: 75% vested
  - Year 4: 100% vested

#### Investment Options
- Target date funds (2025-2065)
- Index funds (S&P 500, Total Market, International)
- Bond funds (Government, Corporate, High Yield)
- Company stock (limited to 10% of portfolio)
- Self-directed brokerage option

### Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)

- **Discount**: 15% off market price
- **Contribution**: 1-10% of salary
- **Purchase Periods**: Quarterly
- **Holding Period**: 1 year recommended for tax benefits

## Health Savings Account (HSA)

Available to employees enrolled in HDHP:
- **Company Contribution**: $500/year (prorated for mid-year enrollment)
- **Employee Contribution**: Up to IRS limit ($4,150 individual, $8,300 family for 2024)
- **Catch-Up Contribution**: Additional $1,000 if age 55+
- **Investment Option**: Available once balance exceeds $1,000
- **Rollover**: Unlimited, funds never expire

## Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA)

### Healthcare FSA
- **Annual Limit**: $3,050 (2024)
- **Eligible Expenses**: Medical, dental, vision not covered by insurance
- **Carryover**: Up to $610 to next year
- **Use-It-Or-Lose-It**: Remaining balance forfeited

### Dependent Care FSA
- **Annual Limit**: $5,000 per household
- **Eligible Expenses**: Daycare, after-school care, elder care
- **No Carryover**: Must be used within plan year

## Enrollment and Changes

### Open Enrollment
- **When**: November 1-15 annually
- **Effective Date**: January 1
- **How**: Via HR Portal or Benefits Hotline

### Qualifying Life Events
Changes allowed within 30 days of:
- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of child
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in employment status

### How to Enroll
1. Log into HR Portal (hr.acme-corp.com)
2. Navigate to Benefits section
3. Review plan options and costs
4. Select coverage for yourself and dependents
5. Designate beneficiaries
6. Confirm elections
