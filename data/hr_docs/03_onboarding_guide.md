# New Employee Onboarding Guide - Acme Corporation

## Welcome to Acme Corporation!

Congratulations on joining the Acme team! This guide will help you navigate your first days, weeks, and months at the company. We're excited to have you on board and committed to setting you up for success.

## Before Your First Day

### What to Expect

One week before your start date, you will receive:
- **Welcome Email**: From HR with first-day instructions
- **Equipment Information**: Details about your laptop and equipment
- **Paperwork Link**: Online forms to complete before Day 1
- **Parking/Access Information**: Badge pickup and parking details

### Pre-Start Paperwork

Complete these forms before your first day via the Onboarding Portal:
1. **I-9 Employment Eligibility**: Bring original documents on Day 1
2. **W-4 Tax Withholding**: Federal tax elections
3. **State Tax Form**: If applicable
4. **Direct Deposit Authorization**: Bank account information
5. **Emergency Contact Information**: Two contacts required
6. **Background Check Authorization**: If not completed during hiring

### What to Bring on Day 1

- Government-issued photo ID (for I-9 verification)
- Social Security card or birth certificate (for I-9 verification)
- Voided check or bank letter (for direct deposit)
- Personal items for your workspace

## Your First Week

### Required Training (Complete by Day 5)

All new employees must complete these online courses:
1. **Information Security Awareness** (45 minutes)
2. **Code of Conduct** (30 minutes)
3. **Anti-Harassment Training** (60 minutes)
4. **Data Privacy Fundamentals** (30 minutes)
5. **Safety in the Workplace** (20 minutes)

Access training at: learning.acme-corp.com

### Key Meetings

Your manager will schedule:
- **1:1 with Manager**: Daily check-ins during Week 1
- **Team Introduction**: Meet all team members
- **Stakeholder Meetings**: Key people you'll work with
- **Buddy Introduction**: Your assigned onboarding buddy

### Systems Access

Request access to systems you need via IT Portal:
- Jira (project management)
- Confluence (documentation)
- GitHub (code repository)
- Salesforce (if customer-facing)
- Department-specific tools

### Onboarding Buddy Program

You'll be paired with an onboarding buddy who will:
- Answer day-to-day questions
- Show you around the office
- Introduce you to colleagues
- Have lunch with you during Week 1
- Be available via Slack for quick questions

## Your First 30 Days

### 30-Day Goals

Work with your manager to establish:
- Learning objectives
- Key projects to shadow
- People to meet
- Skills to develop
- Initial deliverables

### Required Activities

- [ ] Complete all required training
- [ ] Set up benefits enrollment (within 30 days)
- [ ] Complete 401(k) enrollment
- [ ] Review and sign employee handbook acknowledgment
- [ ] Complete department-specific onboarding
- [ ] Schedule 30-day check-in with manager

### Benefits Enrollment Deadline

You have 30 days from your start date to:
- Enroll in medical, dental, and vision insurance
- Select life insurance beneficiaries
- Set up FSA or HSA contributions
- Review and elect voluntary benefits

Enroll at: hr.acme-corp.com/benefits

### Performance Expectations

During your first 30 days:
- Focus on learning, not delivering
- Ask questions freely
- Take notes during meetings
- Build relationships with teammates
- Understand team processes and tools

## Your First 60 Days

### 60-Day Milestones

By day 60, you should:
- Understand your role and responsibilities
- Know key stakeholders and their roles
- Be contributing to team projects
- Have completed all required training
- Feel comfortable with daily tools and processes

### Expanding Your Network

Schedule coffee chats or brief meetings with:
- Cross-functional partners
- Senior team members
- People in roles you're curious about
- Your skip-level manager (manager's manager)

### Feedback Conversations

- **30-Day Check-in**: Informal conversation with manager
- **60-Day Review**: More structured feedback session
- Topics: What's going well, areas for growth, questions, support needed

## Your First 90 Days

### 90-Day Review

At 90 days, you'll have a formal review covering:
- Progress against initial goals
- Strengths demonstrated
- Areas for development
- Feedback from colleagues
- Goals for next quarter

### End of Probationary Period

The 90-day probationary period concludes with:
- Confirmation of employment status
- Full access to all benefits
- Eligibility for internal transfers (after 6 months)
- Performance bonus eligibility (if applicable)

### Setting Long-Term Goals

Work with your manager to establish:
- Quarterly objectives
- Professional development plan
- Career growth discussions
- Project assignments

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Payroll

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Bi-weekly on Fridays. First paycheck may be partial depending on start date.

**Q: How do I view my pay stubs?**
A: Log into hr.acme-corp.com and navigate to Payroll > Pay Statements.

**Q: When will my direct deposit start?**
A: Usually by your second paycheck. First check may be paper.

### Benefits

**Q: When can I enroll in benefits?**
A: Within 30 days of your start date. Coverage begins the 1st of the following month.

**Q: Can I add dependents later?**
A: Only during Open Enrollment (November) or within 30 days of a qualifying life event.

**Q: When can I enroll in 401(k)?**
A: After 90 days of employment. You'll receive enrollment information automatically.

### Time Off

**Q: How do I request time off?**
A: Submit requests through the HR Portal at least 2 weeks in advance.

**Q: Do I accrue PTO during my first 90 days?**
A: Yes, you accrue PTO from Day 1, but usage requires manager approval during probation.

**Q: What if I'm sick during my first week?**
A: Contact your manager and HR immediately. Sick leave is available from Day 1.

### IT and Equipment

**Q: What if I have technical issues?**
A: Contact IT Help Desk at it-help@acme-corp.com or call ext. 4357 (HELP).

**Q: Can I install software on my laptop?**
A: Only approved software. Request new software through IT Portal.

**Q: What's the WiFi password?**
A: Use your network credentials. Guest WiFi: AcmeGuest / Welcome123
